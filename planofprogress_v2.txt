The Prime Driective:
Increase your Survival Potential, or in other words, preserve your genetic legacy.

The five core values of the Plan of Progress:

1) Truth.
2) Moral Goodness.
3) Family.
4) Respect for life.
5) Transcendence.

The five points of the Plan of Progress:

1) Understand myself as an evolved being and become a conscious agent of evolution.
2) Align my will with the long-term survival of humanity to increase human survival potential while preserving my genetic legacy.
3) Participate in the bearing and raising of the next generation.
4) Protect to the fullest extent possible the sacred right of all living beings to fulfill the Prime Directive.
5) Strive for transcendence by changing the world, and help others to do the same.
